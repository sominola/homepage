— Hello! Who I'm? I'm an American hip hop recording artist and record producer, influenced by Kid Cudi, M.I.A., Kanye West, Toro y Moi, and T.I., and my 2013 Owl Pharaoh was compared by Spin magazine to that of Cudi's Man on the Moon II: The Legend of Mr. Rager.
